About CatKoi

A small server with a personal start that turned into a community project.

CatKoi started with one simple idea: setting up a modded server for my girlfriend so we could have our own world to play in together. What began as a private little project quickly turned into something we wanted to share with more people — a relaxed place where friends could build, explore, and actually enjoy the game together.

Now the goal is to grow that into a real community. Not a giant chaotic network, but a server full of people who want to hang out, build cool stuff, help each other, and make the world feel alive. If that sounds like your kind of server, you’re exactly who we want here.

How it started

One setup, one world, and a plan to make something fun and personal instead of just joining another random server.

What it is now

A modded Create-focused SMP with a live map, a growing Discord, and a bunch of room for more people to leave their mark.

What we want

A chill community of players who want to stick around, build big things, and make the server feel like home.
